# Yoga4Autism

**Unlocking the Gift Within**

Yoga4Autism is a yoga and mindfulness platform specifically designed for autistic children, young people and adults — and for the families, carers and educators supporting them. The philosophy is simple: every person on the spectrum has extraordinary gifts. Yoga and mindfulness are tools to help unlock them.

## Philosophy

Autism is not a deficit. It is a different way of experiencing the world — one that often comes with profound sensitivity, creativity, pattern recognition and depth of focus. Yoga4Autism provides structured, sensory-aware movement and mindfulness practices that work *with* the autistic nervous system rather than against it.

## What Yoga4Autism Offers

- **Sensory-adapted yoga flows** — sequences designed to be safe and enjoyable for sensory differences
- **Visual cue cards** — illustrated pose guides that don't rely on verbal instruction
- **Carer and educator guides** — how to support practice at home and in the classroom
- **Breathing techniques** — calming breathwork proven to reduce anxiety and meltdowns
- **Parent community** — shared experiences, tips and mutual support
- **OASIS Omniverse integration** — karma earned for practice, digital avatar companion support

## OASIS Integration

Yoga4Autism is part of the OASIS Omniverse ecosystem. Completing practice sessions earns OASIS karma, which carries across all connected apps — giving young people on the spectrum a positive, achievement-based digital identity they can be proud of.
